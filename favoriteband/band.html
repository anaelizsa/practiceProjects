ONE OK ROCK

A Japanese rock band formed in 2005.
The band currently consists of vocalist Takahiro Moriuchi,
guitarist Toru Yamashita, bassist Ryota Kohama, and drummer
Tomoya Kanki. Former members include drummer Yu Koyanagi and
guitarist Alexander "Alex" Reimon Onizawa. They play varied
styles of music, with songs ranging from alternative rock and
emo to post-hardcore and pop punk.
